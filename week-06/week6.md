After completing Sprint One and presenting our progress to the client, our team discussed the feedback we received and developed an action plan for the next stage of development. 
The client was happy with the progress we had made so far, but they also suggested some improvements that could make the system clearer and more effective.
As a team, we reviewed what worked well during the sprint and identified areas that could be improved. 
One of the main priorities moving forward is to refine some of the user interface elements and improve the way attendance information is displayed for administrators. 
We also plan to improve feedback messages for users when they register their attendance.
Our action plan focuses on building on the work completed in Sprint One, improving usability, and making sure the system continues to meet the client’s requirements.
By reflecting on the feedback and planning the next steps carefully, we aim to make the application more polished and reliable in the next sprint.
