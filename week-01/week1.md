Week 1 – Team Roles Reflection
In week 1, we completed a personality questionnaire to understand the type of role we naturally take in a team. 
My results showed that my main roles are Diplomat and Facilitator.
The Diplomat role focuses on communication and maintaining good relationships within the group. 
I think this reflects how I usually work in a team because I try to listen to everyone’s ideas and make sure discussions stay positive. 
I also tend to notice when people may disagree and try to keep the atmosphere calm so the team can stay focused on the task.
The Facilitator role is about helping the team stay organised and making sure everyone contributes. 
I feel this is something I naturally try to do by encouraging people to share their ideas and making sure the group stays on track.
One weakness mentioned in the results is that Diplomats can sometimes hesitate to take a strong side in decisions. After reading this,
I realised it’s something I should work on by being more confident in sharing my opinion when it helps the team move forward.
In this project, I hope to support the team by helping maintain good communication while also contributing technically as a Front-End Developer.
I believe combining these strengths will help the team work more effectively throughout the project.
