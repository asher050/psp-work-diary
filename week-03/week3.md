In week 3 our team discussed how we would approach the development of the project. We decided to follow an Agile approach, which allows us to work in short development cycles and regularly review our progress. 
This method helps us stay flexible and make improvements based on feedback from our client and academic consultant.
To prepare for the sprint week, we agreed to divide the work based on each team member’s strengths. 
My role as the Front-End Developer is to design and implement the login page and the attendance registration interface.
Other team members focused on backend development and the admin functionality.
During the sprint week we plan to work closely together, communicate regularly, and test each part of the system as it is developed. 
By working in smaller stages and reviewing our progress frequently, we aim to ensure the application functions correctly and meets the client’s requirements.
